ABOUT

FOUNDER

1949 Kobe, Japan was in shambles following WWII. Many Japanese youth lost hope fro the future. Our founder,Kihachiro Onisuka, knew he had to do something to uplift the younger generation. Onisuka decided to smart making athletic footwaer that would promote an active lifestyle among the youth.

Since our beginning, ASICS has been built on the philosophy Sound Mind, Sound Body™. Our founder, Kihachiro Onitsuka, believed physical activity was an essential part of overall wellbeing. He started ASICS to make sportswear that would support every athlete, inspire movement and encourage everyone to have an active lifestyle.

OK Basketball Shoe

The OK Basketball Shoe was the start of it all for the brand as the very first ASICS product. At this time basketball shoes were difficult to make, but that did not deter Onitsuka - instead, it inspired him. He gave early prototypes to basketball leagues in Kobe for wear testing. It was refined and released to the public in the spring of 1950.
